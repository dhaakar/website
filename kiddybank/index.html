Kiddy Bank
Virtual Savings Account for your kids

Create savings accounts for your kids without stepping out of your home. You be the bank manager for your kids account. Set the interest rate, add transactions and show your kids the power of compound interest.

Please Note : This app does not transact real money. It is a book keeping app to easily manage your kids money

Features :

  1. Create unlimited savings accounts for all your kids
  2. Select an avatar theme for each account to your kid's preference
  3. Automatic interest credit entry every month end based on the configured interest percentage
  4. Chart view to see the historical trends of the money saved
  5. Table view to list all the transactions
  6. Biometric authentication to add a new transaction
  7. Login with your google id across multiple devices to sync your data automatically
